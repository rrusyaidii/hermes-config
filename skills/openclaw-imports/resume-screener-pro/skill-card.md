## Description: <br>
Resume Screener Pro supports a four-stage recruiting workflow for resume screening, interview design, interview evaluation, and final candidate recommendations with ATS checks and structured follow-up questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[wuritu](https://clawhub.ai/user/wuritu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
HR recruiting leads, recruiters, and interviewers use this skill to screen resumes against a job description, design structured interview questions, evaluate interview responses, and prepare final candidate recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill is intended for recruiting workflows and may process candidate resumes, interview notes, and other personal data. <br>
Mitigation: Use it only when authorized to process candidate data, minimize unnecessary personal information, and follow applicable hiring and privacy controls. <br>
Risk: AI-generated screening scores or recommendations could influence hiring decisions without sufficient review. <br>
Mitigation: Require human review before any hiring decision and avoid relying on protected characteristics or unsupported inferences. <br>
