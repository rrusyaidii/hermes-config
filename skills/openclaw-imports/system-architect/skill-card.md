This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to plan new applications, refactor existing systems, choose technology stacks, define project structure, and produce architecture documentation. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill can activate on broad design or structure-review requests where the user may not want architecture-level guidance. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the user wants architecture-level guidance when a request is ambiguous. <br>
Risk: Architecture recommendations and generated scaffolding may not match project-specific security, scalability, or operational requirements. <br>
Mitigation: Review recommendations against the target environment, threat model, and team standards before implementation. <br>
