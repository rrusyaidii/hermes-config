Risk: Architecture guidance may be incomplete or unsuitable for a specific jurisdiction, site, or structure. <br>
Mitigation: Verify recommendations with qualified local professionals before relying on them for structural safety, permits, zoning, code compliance, or construction decisions. <br>
Risk: Conceptual design advice could be mistaken for construction-ready documentation. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill output as general design guidance and require professional review before producing permit, bid, or construction documents. <br>
